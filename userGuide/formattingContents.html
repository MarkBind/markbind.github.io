    Relevant Tips & Tricks

    Tip: Escaping Characters

    For Markdown syntax: To display a literal character that are normally used for Markdown formatting, add a backslash (\) in front of the character.

    CODE:

    \* item 1

* item 1

    OUTPUT:

    * item 1
